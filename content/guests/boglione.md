Elena Boglione is a professor at the University of Turin in Italy, one of the oldest universities in Europe. Since the early stages of TMD research, Elena and her group in Turin have been at the forefront of theoretical investigations of TMDs. Her publications include some of the world-first extractions of TMDs.
